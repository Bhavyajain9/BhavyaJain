Business Hotel

A Business Hotel is an establishment where the main client segment stems reason for travel is business, Jems & jewellery in this case.
As Jaipur is one of the largest exporters of Gems & Jewellery all over the world, a private developer is looking to establish a compact business hotel on the site at it park road, Sitapura, Jaipur. The site gives various opportunities in terms of being an important urban node as well as in close proximity to the business spine (Tonk road) and airport. The idea behind the proposed business hotel will be that it'll be catering to only a specific category of business zone, i.e., the gems and jewellery sector.

ISBT Sindhi Camp(ANDC 2020-21)

Travel, Commute, Business, Education and Economy, all of this has come to a pause due to the current pandemic. Conversely, due to the upsurge, the public mobility space playing a vital role in today's situation, is inevitable by human activity and has lead to uncontrollable mobility at every square mile which has given rise to increase in crowded spaces and making them hotspots for transmission of diseases and hence caged people inside their homes.
Transformation of such spaces and change in idea of public mobility is the need of the hour, in pursuit of livable cities, towns and villages starting with the rudimentary i.e. mobility spaces.
This project is one of the possible answers to an alternative way of thinking to socialize in high density areas.

Health and Wellness Centre

Health refers to physical, mental, and social well-being; wellness aims to enhance well-being.
HWC are the cornerstone of urban health services- a first port of call to a qualified doctor of the public sector in urban areas for the sick and those who directly report or referred for curative, preventive and primitive health care.
The services envisaged at the HWC level will include early identification, basic management, counselling, ensuring treatment, adherence, follow up care, ensuing continuity of care by appropriate referrals, optimal home and community follow up and health promotion and prevention for the expanded range of services. Along with the hospital, a yoga and gym centre with the doctor’s residence is also to be proposed.

Farmhouse Landscape Design

The given project is a farmhouse, located in Surana village, 60 kms from Jaipur. The farmhouse is for a furniture manufacturer, who want to spend weekends there with his immediate family and sometime with extended family and friends. They also plan to let their buyers stay here, both Indian and international clients.
The idea for the landscape is of a weekend home that can cater to small and large groups, keeping in mind the people of all age groups and different interests. Considering the very potential of open spaces, semi open, amalgamation of open and closed spaces, the site has been given with the motive to integrate landscape spaces which blends with the architecture of the space.

Krishi Vigyan Kendra

India is agriculture-based economy where most of the farmers are illiterate thereby making it difficult for them to take advantage of the government schemes at central and state level. Krishi Vigyan Kendra is a district level farm science centre which can help in speedy transfer of technology to the farmer’s field by building bridges between research institutes and the farmers. It aims at technology assessment, demonstration and its dissemination through training of farmers, rural youth and extension personnel. They’ve started the instructional units for not only imparting the skills but also for providing the critical inputs as per the demand and need of the farmer. Their responsibilities include on-farm testing, front-line demonstration, capacity building, multi-sector support and advisory services.

Electronic Vehicular Charging Station (EVCS)

To address growing CO2 emissions and air pollution in cities, Electric vehicles are cars and other forms of mobility that use an electric motor as their main source of propulsion, rather than a conventional engine will be used. Battery electric vehicles are all-electric cars that rely on their batteries as the only source of energy. Hybrid electric vehicles and plug in hybrid electric vehicles combine an electric drive with a conventional fuel engine.
Speed, availability and reliability of charging infrastructure are currently the biggest potential deterrents to buying an electric car.

The Farmstay

A farmstay is essentially accommodation available on a working farm. With growing popularity of homestays, Farmstays are blossoming across the country. Offering its guest unique experiences, it’s one of the best ways to enjoy nature at its best away from the hustle and bustle of the city. They blend ecotourism, sustainable agriculture as well as environmental education. Away from the privileges of the city the stays are with all modern facilities and amenities. Usually run by the family the owners offer domestic food and extend a warm hospitality.
The site being in Thekkady, Kerala has a warm & humid climate and very fewer seasonal & diurnal variations and requires an elongated planform and a sloped roof in order to be climate responsive.
